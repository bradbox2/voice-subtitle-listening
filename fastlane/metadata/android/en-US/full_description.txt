Subtitle listening fork of Voice for language practice.
Not the official Voice app.

Use your local audiobooks and subtitles together:

- Load same-folder `.srt` files or subtitle files picked through SAF / `content://`
- Tap a subtitle to jump playback
- Highlight the current subtitle line while listening
- Star lines for review and practice
- Keep offline audiobook playback when subtitles are not available
- Use Voice's original playback modes for sequential play, single-track repeat, shuffle, and folder repeat

Made for DRM-free local files, no ads, and no account required.
Best suited for short practice clips around 3-8 minutes. For the most stable subtitle sync, prefer clips no longer than 10 minutes.
Clicking a subtitle re-aligns playback from that sentence, but it does not globally re-time the entire subtitle file.
